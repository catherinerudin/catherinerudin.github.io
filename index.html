I'm a fifth-year PhD student in Linguistics at UC Santa Cruz, working on semantics, pragmatics and what happens when they meet.

I'm extremely contactable: drudin@ucsc.edu

I co-founded (and used to coordinate) the SPLAP! reading group, which focuses on semantics, pragmatics and philosophy of language.

My advisor is Pranav Anand, and I'm a member of Adrian Brasoveanu's Language, Logic and Cognition lab.

I'm currently TAing Syntactic Structures. My office hours are Mondays from 1-2pm in Stevenson 238.
